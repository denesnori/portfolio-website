Interests

What I am up to If not coding?

Meetups

I love the meetups. I can't keep track of the number of meetup groups i signed up for. I have already met many highly motivated people changing careers, wanting to do something good or just improve their knowledge.

Code Your Future

I have recently started volunteering for Code Your Future, a coding school for refugees. They teach CSS/HTML, Javascript and backend technologies. I help them to deliver their backend module.

Volunteering

Coming from a non Computer Science background, volunteering for coding related conferences gives me an amzing opportunity to broaden my horizon, forge new friendships and give something back to the developer community. Last year I volunteered for DevoxxUK and ODSC London.

Hiking

I fell in love with hiking when I walked the El Camino ( the French Way) in 2014. I walked the the Potuguese Camino. After I moved to England, I decided to walk the Scottish lonf distance hiking trails. I have walked the Great Glenn way and parts of the West Highland Way.

Tennis

I love playing and watching tennis. I even had a taste of being a chair umpire. when I volunteered at junior tennis tournaments. One of the great gifts of living in London was to be able to attend the Wimbledon tennis tournament last year. It was a dream come true!

English

Moving to the UK less than a year ago, it is still a long way to till i can master the language and the pronounciation. I manly learn new words and phrases with anki cards. Every conversation helps me to improve my English. Come and talk to me.
